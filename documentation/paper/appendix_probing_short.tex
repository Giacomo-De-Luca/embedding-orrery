\section{Probe Settings and Per-Dimension Results}
\label{app:probes}

\paragraph{Probe settings.}
All probes share one $80/20$ split (seed $42$) on per-feature $z$-scored
activations. \textsc{Ridge}: $\ell_2$ linear regression, $\alpha=1$.
\textsc{SVR}: support-vector regression, RBF kernel, $C=1$. \textsc{MLP}: one
hidden layer ($256$, ReLU, dropout $0.1$), Adam (lr $10^{-3}$, weight decay
$10^{-4}$), MSE loss, batch size $64$, up to $100$ epochs with patience-$10$
early stopping on validation loss. \textsc{Mass-mean}: the closed-form
unit-normalised difference-of-means direction $\bm{\mu}_{+}-\bm{\mu}_{-}$ of
\citet{marks2023geometry} on a median split, scored by projection (reported as
Spearman $\rho$, an uncalibrated ranking). \textsc{Logistic}: $\ell_2$ logistic
regression ($C=1$, up to $1000$ iterations) on a median split of the norm,
reported as classification accuracy. Ridge/SVR/MLP report $R^2$. All figures are
the \emph{last} hidden layer (MiniLM L6, EmbeddingGemma L24) --- i.e.\ the model
output embedding. \textsc{Concreteness-40k} is the full Brysbaert set
($39{,}954$ words) on the final pooled MiniLM embedding.
